About

I'm a dancer, actor, and singer based in Boston. I'm a non-binary performer, and I'm hungry for performing opportunities that allow me to explore and celebrate the human experience outside of the bounds of gender, which I find so limiting. There's something about climbing into a character on a stage that gives me a sense of peace and freedom in my body as a gender nonconforming person that I just don't get out there in the "real world."

I'm originally from just north of New York City, where I trained with Broadway and Radio City vets in dance, singing, and acting. I'm now Boston-based, and have loved being a part of this growing community.
