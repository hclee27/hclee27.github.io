H. C. Lee

Dancer: from freedom comes elegance

H.C. is a dancer, actor, and singer based in Boston. Classical ballet, broadway jazz and tap, and athletic modern and lyrical provide the foundation for their technique as a dancer, and character body awareness as an actor.

A non-binary performer, H.C. can capture characters across the gender spectrum, and most recently has appeared as:

  • Male Ensemble in New Rep's "Oliver"
  • Male Ensemble in The Company Theatre's "Paragon Park: The Musical"
  • Charity in the Weston Friendly Society's "Sweet Charity"
